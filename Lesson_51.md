### If he, or someone like him, were alive today, he might be able to tell us what to expect in the next 50 years.  
  
"疑问词 + to do" 可以视为名词性从句的缩略形式  
  
1. 宾语从句  
They will hardly know which facts to select from ... -> They will hardly know which facts they could select from ...  
  
..., he might be able to tell us what to expect in the next 50 years. -> ..., he might be able to tell us what we should expect in the next 50 years.  
  
2. 主语从句  
Who to turn to is what she wants to know. -> Who she should turn to is what she wants to know.  
  
3. 表语从句  
The problem she faces is which way to take. -> The problem she faces is which way she should take.  
  
4. 同位语从句  
The problem which course to take worried Lucy for hours. -> The problem which course she should take worried Lucy for hours.
